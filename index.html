About Sovereign Debt Digest

The Sovereign Debt Digest is Kepler Karst's weekly analysis of the most significant developments in sovereign debt markets, restructuring cases, and international financial law.

Our team monitors over 50 leading sources including Financial Times, Bloomberg, Reuters, IMF, World Bank, and specialized publications to bring you the most relevant insights each week.

Using our proprietary scoring system, we identify the top 20 most impactful developments, providing concise analysis of restructuring progress, debt sustainability metrics, innovative instruments, and forward-looking implications.

This digest serves as an essential resource for legal professionals, financial institutions, government officials, and anyone with an interest in sovereign debt markets and international financial law.
